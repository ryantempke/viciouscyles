Custom Bikes and Quality Equipment

At Covenant Bicycles, we specialize in building custom bikes that are tailored to your needs and preferences. Our team of experienced technicians will work with you to design a bike that fits you like a glove and performs the way you want it to. We also carry a wide selection of industry-leading accessories and top-quality equipment to help you get the most out of your cycling experience.

Service You Can Count On

Our philosophy is that choosing a bike is about more than just the bike itself. It's about choosing the people who will build your bicycle, help you get comfortable on it, and provide service down the road as you need it. That's why we're committed to providing the best service possible, every step of the way. Whether you need a tune-up, a repair, or just some advice on your next ride, we're here to help.
